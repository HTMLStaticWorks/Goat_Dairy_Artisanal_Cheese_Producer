Ecosystem Stewardship

Regenerative Farming Philosophy

100% Clean Energy

Our dairy processing machines, cooling units, and barns are fully run by localized solar and wind energy grids.

Zero Waste Water

Whey output from cheesemaking is recycled into organic fertilizers or pasture nourishment, keeping waterways completely pure.

Rotational Grazing

We move our herds daily, preventing soil erosion, stimulating biodiversity, and allowing meadows to recover organically.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is raw goat cheese safe?

Absolutely! Our raw cheeses are aged for a minimum of 60 days in compliance with federal guidelines. The natural enzymes remain intact, providing deeper flavors and helpful probiotic support.

What is the difference between goat milk and cow milk?

Goat milk contains smaller fat globules and lacks the A1 casein proteins, which makes it much easier to digest, especially for people with mild dairy sensitivities.

Do you ship orders nationwide?

Yes, our online ordering system supports cold-insulated express shipping. We pack all cheese wheels in compostable wool insulations with gel ice packs.
