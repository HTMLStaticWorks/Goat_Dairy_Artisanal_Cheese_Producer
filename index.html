Our Heritage

From Clover Meadows to Artisanal Boards

At Capra, our journey began over three generations ago with a single dairy herd and an unwavering passion for high-welfare dairy farming. We believe that premium artisanal cheese starts with the soils, the climate, and most importantly, the happiness of our free-roaming Alpine and LaMancha goats.

By feeding our goats wild pasture grass and native clover, their milk carries distinct botanical notes that define the signature flavors of Capra cheeses. No preservatives, no fillers—just pure, cold-filtered natural goodness.

Artisanal Mastery

The Cheese Crafting Process

We combine centuries-old European dairy traditions with modern laboratory standards to produce premium culinary blocks.

01

Milking & Filtering

Milked fresh at dawn and immediately chilled to maintain organic integrity.

02

Slow Coagulation

Curdled naturally using biological vegetarian rennets over 24 hours.

03

Molding & Salting

Ladled by hand into custom molds and salted with hand-ground sea salt.

04

Cellar Aging

Aged in controlled microclimates to enrich botanical flavor dynamics.

Nutritional Advantages

Why Choose Goat Milk Cheese?

Goat milk is naturally structured with shorter fatty acid chains and smaller fat globules than cow's milk, making it highly bio-digestible and offering a luxurious flavor profile.

Easier Digestion

Contains significantly lower A1 casein proteins, lowering bloating and stomach sensitivites.

Rich in Nutrients

Loaded with calcium, phosphorus, vitamin A, and healthy medium-chain fatty acids.

Complex Flavor Profile

Provides a signature tart finish and rich finish coveted by gourmet culinary chefs worldwide.
